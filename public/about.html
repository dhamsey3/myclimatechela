About us

Our voices. Our planet. Our future.

My Climate Definition is a space for stories and conversations about climate change through the lens of lived experience. Climate isn’t abstract — it shapes the way we live, work, and connect every day.

We share how our values, choices, and everyday actions — alongside the systems we create — impact our planet and our communities. By telling our stories, we aim to make climate change personal, relatable, and urgent.

This platform is about learning together, inspiring change, and reimagining what’s possible. We want to spark conversations that empower individuals and communities to help build a more sustainable, just, and resilient world — today and for generations to come.

Vision

A world where personal stories and collective experiences inspire action that builds sustainable, resilient, and just communities for present and future generations.

Mission

To connect daily human choices to global climate impacts and inspire people to reimagine and act toward a more sustainable and resilient future.

Our core values

  • Community — Change begins with people coming together, learning from each other, and supporting collective action.
  • Sustainability — Our words and actions are guided by the need to protect the planet and build systems that nurture rather than harm.
  • Justice — We recognize climate impacts are unequal and believe solutions must be fair and inclusive.
  • Hope & Action — We share stories to raise awareness and inspire courage, creativity, and action toward a better future.
